Water is a clear, odorless liquid essential for life, making up about 60% of an adult human's body weight. Its key benefits include:

1. Maintaining proper hydration
2. Regulating body temperature
3. Facilitating waste removal
4. Supporting digestion and nutrient absorption
5. Aiding in energy production

Additionally, water benefits skin health, supports kidney function, enhances exercise performance, and contributes to cognitive function and weight management. It also
 helps regulate blood pressure, supports detoxification, boosts the immune system, and maintains joint and respiratory health.
